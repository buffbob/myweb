About Page

Young kid from the south loved Colorado while visiting on vacation and decides this is place for him. Can't beat the climate, lack of bugs, and oh, those mountains. Eventually got a college degree in Chemistry from CU in 1993. This led obviously to... starting a pizza shop in Nederland and a decade of experience in carpentry.

A need for less human contact led to a the purchase of small ranch in SE Colorado. After raising bison for a dozen years, an old back injury convinced me to try something new. Oh, and there was this girl too. So back to the Front Range but now with a family. I tried Real Estate but not really my cup of tea.

Starting a biz, carpentry, and mostly ranching has honed what I think of as my best asset- problem solving. So I decided to learn 'programming'. I learned it from the internet. It was hard.

So i ain't got no formal education- there you go. But I hope you can see from my projects that I took this endeavor quite seriously. I think my aforementioned education(University of Internet) highlights my abilities as a problem solver. I came across many hurdles with little guidance. Yet here I am confident that my hard won abilities will score me a new future.

Now living in Golden. Playing lots of tennis with my daughter and trying to keep my border collie entertained.
